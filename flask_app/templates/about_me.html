About Me

Yup thats me, you're probably wondering how I got here...


My interest in programming began in my small hometown's high school where I took a CS elective. Intimidation set in from being the only girl in the class and my record was tainted with a D from CS due to me skipping class. As a stable career was insisted on by my parents, that is what I pursued until I was done feeling unfulfilled and had gained the confidence to learn software development. After messing around with HTML and CSS for a year, I joined a full stack development bootcamp.


Providing a win-win situation is always the goal where an organization is efficient and successful while making a positive impact in the user's experience.


With a Bachelor's in Healthcare Administration and a decade in healthcare operations, I have customer service and problem-solving at the center of any work I do. It is exciting to bring my eye for efficiency and process improvement into programming!
